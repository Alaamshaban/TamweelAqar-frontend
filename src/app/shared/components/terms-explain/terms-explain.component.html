Terms Explained

Home Price

Is the value of the house at date of mortgage.
Home prices are further evaluated by the mortgage company through approved real estate appraisers.

Loan Amount:

Is the price of the home minus the down payment paid by the borrower at time of mortgage.

Required Salary

CBE regulations require that monthly loan repayments do not exceed 40% of the borrower's monthly income.
Companies typically accept forms of income other than salary, such as income on certificates of deposit held at a bank.

Minimum Down Payment

Is the minimum accepted down payment paid by the borrower as their contribution towards the purchase of the property.

Maximum Mortgage Period

The maximum number of months that a mortgage company will allow the borrower to repay the loan amount over.
Lengthier mortgage period lead to lower monthly payments.

Admin Fees

Mortgage companies typically charge borrowers a fee in order to issue them the mortgage financing.

Monthly Amount

Monthly payments are the amount to be paid by the borrower to the mortgage company in return for the financing.
Monthly payments are higher with shorter period and higher value mortgage options.
